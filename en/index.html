Frequently Asked Questions

Will WAC Shield slow down my website?

No. It was developed with extreme performance in mind. Blocking occurs at the WordPress init level, saving server resources that would otherwise be spent processing a fake login attempt.

What if I get blocked by mistake?

You can easily add your IP to the Whitelist. If you are blocked after too many password attempts, simply switch networks (use your mobile data) or access the database via your hosting panel as explained in our documentation.
